TDEE Calculator

This calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation to calculate its output. The equation it self is source from Medscape.com The equation itself follows different formulas depending on the sex of a person.

Male = (10*weight [kg]) + (6.25*height [cm]) - (5*age [years]) + 5

Female = (10*weight [kg]) + (6.25*height [cm]) - (5*age [years]) - 161.

Further more you can add a scale factor according to the activity level. Sedentary *1.2, Lightly active *1.375, Moderately active *1.55, Active *1.725, Very active *1.9.
